<h2>Academic Year 2026 Secondary One Registration</h2>
<p>To all students who will join New Town Secondary School (NTSS) as a Secondary
One student in 2026, congratulations and welcome!
<br>
<br><strong>Online Registration – by 21 December 2025</strong>
<br>Parents/Guardians of all 2026 Secondary One students will receive instructions
on the registration process&nbsp;via the Parents Gateway (PG).
<br>There is no need for students to report in-person to the school before
2 January 2026. To confirm your child’s/ward’s enrolment at New Town Secondary
School, parents/guardians must click “<strong>Acknowledge</strong>” in
the Parents Gateway (PG) form <strong>by 21 December 2025 (Sunday)</strong>,<strong> 5.00 pm</strong>.
The 2026 class allocation will be available from 26 December 2025 (Friday)
by clicking on the "CONTACTS" tab at the bottom of the PG home page.</p>
<p><strong>Medical Appeal Exercise – by 22 December 2025</strong>
<br>If your child/ward requires school transfer due to serious medical conditions
or severe physical impairments, please bring along the supporting documents
to New Town Secondary School for submission <strong>by 22 December 2025 (Monday)</strong>,<strong> 12.00 pm</strong>.</p>
<p><strong>Financial Assistance Scheme (FAS)</strong>
<br>If you wish to apply for the MOE FAS, you may find out more information
from the MOE website <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/financial-matters/financial-assistance" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>https://www.moe.gov.sg/financial-matters/financial-assistance</u></a>.
Once the application is approved, parents will be notified and can collect
the textbooks and uniform from the vendors in the school. Some parents
might experience delay in the collection of textbooks and uniform due to
the time taken for the approval of the FAS. Your child/ward may report
to school in his/her primary school uniform if the collection is after
2 January 2026.</p>

<p><strong>First Day of School</strong>
<br>The first day of school is on <strong>2 January 2026 (Friday)</strong>.
All Secondary One students are to report before 7.35 am at the school parade
ground in full school uniform (polo t-shirt and school shorts/skirt). Students
who have not purchased the school uniform in time may report in their primary
school uniform.
<br>Our student leaders will guide your child/ward from the foyer to the parade
ground. Parents/guardians will not be allowed into the school premises,
except for drop off at the foyer.</p>
<p><strong>Secondary One Orientation</strong>
<br>All Secondary One students will attend the Secondary One Orientation from <strong>2 to 6 January 2026</strong>.
The aim of the programme is to familiarise students to the new school environment,
as well as to get to know their Form Teachers and classmates better.&nbsp;
<br>There is no need to bring any textbooks during the orientation. Students
are to bring writing materials, water bottle and a story book for reading.
Attendance for the programme is compulsory.</p>
