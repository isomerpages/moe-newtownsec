<h3><strong>2026 Sec 1 Appeal to Transfer to NTSS</strong></h3>
<p><strong>Non-medical Appeal Exercise</strong>
</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>If your child/ward is not posted to New Town Secondary School and you
would like to make an appeal due to non-medical reasons, please fill in
the online form below <strong>by</strong>  <strong>25 December 2025, 12 pm.</strong> Late
submission will not be considered.</p>
<p>Online form: <a href="https://go.gov.sg/2026-s1-appeal-ntss" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>https://go.gov.sg/2026-s1-appeal-ntss</u></a>
</p>
<p>Your child’s/ward’s PSLE score must meet the school's cut-off point of
the posting year. The cut-off points for 2026 Secondary One Posting to
New Town Secondary School are as follows:</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Posting Group 3: AL 20</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Posting Group 2: AL 25</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Transfer will be subject to available vacancies and the school’s admission
criteria.</p>
<ol start="2">
<li>
<p><strong>Students should still report to the posted secondary school on 2 January 2026</strong>.
The application is deemed to be unsuccessful if you do not hear from the
school by 2 January 2026. Schools will not disclose reasons for selection
/ non-selection of students.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For appeals based on <strong>medical reasons</strong>, please request through
the school that your child/ward is posted to <strong>by 22 December 2025, 12 pm</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
