<h2>DEVELOPING DIGITAL LITERACIES @ NTSS</h2>
<h4>Tier 1 Programmes: Workshops to develop students’ digital literacy</h4>
<p><strong>Sec 1: iPad Productivity Skills workshop</strong>
<br>Sec 2 and 3 ICT Ambassadors led a hands-on training workshop to guide
their juniors on using their Personal Learning Device (iPad) effectively
and productively for learning.</p>

<p><strong>Sec 3: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (AI) and AI literacy.</strong>
<br>Students explored the world of AI and prompt engineering, discovering
how to use these tools effectively. They then applied their learning by
using Teachable Machine to design their own image-recognition bots.</p>
