<h1>INTRODUCTION</h1>
<p>The NTSS Table Tennis CCA consists of only the boys’ team as of 2021.
The Table Tennis team aims to:</p>
<p>Promote table tennis as a viable recreational sport for healthy living.
Promote appreciation for the sport and attain basic skills. Promote an
opportunity to experience the sport at a competitive level.</p>
